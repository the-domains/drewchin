Plant GroupPAD

Martin Stigsgaard Studio

Concentration: Environment // Body

Spring 2016

Professor Stigsgaard lead the studio with a demanding yet free form balance of creative inspiration and vigorous craft expectation. The introductory project was a transforming memory box that contained multiple personal memories, inspired by Marcel Duchamp’s “Rrose Sélavy.” The second project was a wearable device that augments how the body interacts with the environment. And the final project of the semester was to design a passive micro-dwelling in an extreme climate.
